About

Built in the boroughs, trusted across the tri-state

MetroVolt Systems is a woman-owned, NABCEP and BICSI-certified low voltage contractor headquartered in Long Island City. Our engineers and field crews understand the unique demands of building in New York, from accelerated schedules to strict DOB inspections and union coordination.

We collaborate with general contractors, property managers, and IT teams to deliver seamless infrastructure that stays future-ready. Every project is backed by detailed as-built documentation, preventative maintenance plans, and dedicated support.
